Dr. Rakesh Maurya

Ph.D., Counselor Education and Supervision

Associate Professor

School Counseling Program

University of North Florida

My research focuses on multicultural issues in counseling, the use of technology in counselor education, and the mental health of minority populations. I am particularly interested in developing culturally sensitive interventions and improving access to care for underserved communities.

My recent work explores the impact of telehealth on the therapeutic alliance and the effectiveness of virtual reality in exposure therapy. I am committed to training the next generation of counselors to be competent, ethical, and compassionate practitioners in an increasingly diverse world.

I serve on the editorial board for the Journal of Multicultural Counseling and Development and am an active member of the American Counseling Association.
